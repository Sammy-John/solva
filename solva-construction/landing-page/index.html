Questions worth asking early.

Is this a full CPM tool?

No. The target is a practical builder scheduler: dependency-driven enough to be useful, simple enough to use every week.

Is it desktop-first?

Yes. The product is designed around a dense schedule table that works best on a desktop or laptop.

What makes it construction-specific?

Task types, supplier/internal grouping, inspection rows, delivery and ordering urgency, comments, and workflow links all map to builder scheduling rather than generic project management.
